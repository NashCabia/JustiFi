About The Game

Overview

JustiFi is an interactive, choice-driven visual novel designed to improve legal literacy among senior high school students. The game places players in real-life scenarios involving youth protection, gender safety, cyber safety, and police encounters, allowing them to make decisions and experience the consequences of their actions.

Unlike traditional learning methods, JustiFi uses branching narratives and decision-based gameplay to simulate real-world situations. Each choice made by the player influences the outcome of the story, guiding them toward either a good or bad ending depending on their understanding of legal concepts.

Through this approach, JustiFi aims to make learning about laws engaging, practical, and memorable, helping students become more informed and responsible individuals in society.

Mission: To provide an engaging and interactive platform that enhances students’ understanding of legal rights, responsibilities, and real-world consequences through gamified learning.

Vision: To become a widely recognized educational tool that promotes legal awareness and critical decision-making among students through immersive digital experiences.

About the Team
